Your back Hurts.
Your Cab Doesn't.

Days and nights on the road. Concrete back, stiff neck, heavy eyes. Sitting kills slowly - but every stop, fuel break and CB call is a chance to move.

Align your axles

Each one already fits into your daily drive.

Wiper hands

Swing before you wipe. Dirty windshield is your signal. Arms left-right-left-right. Loosen up before you clean. Simple as that.

Cabin shake

Turn up the music and shake. Every stop is a chance. Shoulders, arms, upper body - loosen what is tightened. Music makes it real.

Challenge

Tire change

Take a part in the truck driver challenge! Record yourself changing a tire in the fastest time possible and share it with other drivers on social media!

Fuel song

Make the fuel hose sing. Pretend it’s microphone. Whatever’s on the radio, you sing it. Relaxes your face, your jaw, your whole stop. Make your mouth relaxed before hours of CB talks!!

Tire walk

Inflate with your own air. Skip the pump. Use your diaphragm instead. Your breathing muscles get stronger. Your tires get filled.

Road talk

Answer the CB and relax your hands. When someone asks how the road is, your hands come alive. Make light and indicator dance.
